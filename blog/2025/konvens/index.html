konvens2025

September 17, 2025

2025

processing main impressions from the conference

  1. A poster reported over 91% F1-scores for a binary classification between human essays and text generated by an unseen agent (Llama-3-8B) instructed to evade detection.
  2. Würzburg team released dedicated German encoders and decoders – a timely alternative to Schweter2020 models.
  3. Paul Röttger gave an impressive CPSS keynote on social bias in LLMs: https://aclanthology.org/2024.acl-long.816.pdf, exploring inter alia the impact of research design.
