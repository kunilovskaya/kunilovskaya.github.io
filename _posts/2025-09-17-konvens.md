<h2 class="title">processing main impressions from the conference</h2>

1. A poster reported over 91% F1-scores for a binary classification between human essays and text generated by an unseen agent (Llama-3-8B) instructed to evade detection. 
2. Würzburg team released dedicated German encoders and decoders -- a timely alternative to Schweter2020 models.
3. Paul Röttger gave an impressive CPSS keynote on social bias in LLMs: <a href="https://aclanthology.org/2024.acl-long.816.pdf" target="blank">https://aclanthology.org/2024.acl-long.816.pdf</a>, exploring inter alia the impact of research design.
